01
Section 01
Submission Guidelines
01
Film duration must be between 1 minute and 30 minutes, including opening and end credits.
02
Film must be an original work. Any copyright infringement will result in immediate disqualification.
03
No obscene language or explicit scenes are permitted under any circumstances.
04
Film is preferably in Tamil. If not in Tamil, English subtitles are mandatory.
05
The entire crew must be current enrolled students of their respective colleges.
06
Minimum recommended resolution: 720p. Accepted formats: MP4, MOV, and standard video formats.
07
Submissions must be made through the official registration link only. No other mode of submission will be accepted.
08
Final submission must arrive on or before the deadline. March 12 2026 - Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
02
Section 02
Eligibility
01
Open to all current college students across India. No restriction on institution or stream.
02
Multiple film submissions are permitted. Each film requires a separate registration and payment of ₹300.
03
Section 03
Screening, Jury & Awards
01
All submissions will be reviewed by the organizing jury and shortlisted films will be selected.
02
Only shortlisted films will be screened in the presence of judges on 14th March 2026, at 1:30 PM.
03
Shortlisted films will be evaluated by Industry Professionals. Their decision is final and binding.
04
Awards will be decided and announced on the event day itself.
